Reel Tokyo, Real Tokyo

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, has served as the setting for numerous films, including both live-action and animated works. This website explores how these two types of films capture the metropolitan city from different perspectives.

Selected films

Your Name.

Your Name. is an animated film released in 2016 and directed by Makoto Shinkai. One day, Taki, a male high school student living in Tokyo, and Mitsuha, a female high school student living in a rural town, mysteriously begin swapping bodies in their dreams. Although they have never met before, they gradually develop a connection by leaving messages for each other and trying to understand each other's lives.

Weathering with You

Weathering with You is another animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai, released three years after Your Name. Hodaka, who runs away to Tokyo, meets Hina, a girl who has a mysterious power to control the weather. As they work together to solve their problems and help people through her special ability, they gradually develop a strong bond. However, they soon face a difficult choice that challenges their relationship and the future of the city.
