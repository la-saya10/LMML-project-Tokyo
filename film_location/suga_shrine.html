Suga Shrine

Film: Your Name.

Address: 5-6 Sugacho, Shinjuku-ku

Angle: Facing north, from top of the stairs

About the Location

The stairs located inside the Suga shrine is the most iconic location in Your Name. where Taki and Mistuha meet for the first time in real life at the end of the film.
