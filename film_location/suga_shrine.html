Otokozaka, the steeper of Suga Shrine's two staircases, gained pilgrimage status following its use in Your Name. (2016).

This staircase is called Otokozaka, meaning "men's slope." It appears in the anime movie Your Name., and many fans visit it to take photos from the same angle seen in the film.

This staircase is called Otokozaka, meaning "men's slope." It appears in the anime movie Your Name., and many fans visit it to take photos from the same angle seen in the film.

Otokozaka is one of two staircases flanking Suga Shrine in Yotsuya, Shinjuku. Its steep gradient and framing toward the Tokyo skyline closely match the composition used in the film's climactic scene, distinguishing it from the shrine itself as the primary pilgrimage target.
